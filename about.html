Our Story

From a simple idea to helping thousands of professionals achieve their career goals

2018

The Beginning

Founded by career coaches who saw talented professionals struggling with outdated resumes. We started with a simple mission: help people present their best professional selves to land better jobs.

2019

ATS Expertise

Recognized the growing importance of ATS systems in hiring. Invested heavily in research and testing to master ATS optimization, achieving our industry-leading 95% pass rate.

2020

Remote Work Revolution

Adapted quickly to the changing job market during the pandemic. Helped thousands of professionals pivot their careers and succeed in the new remote-first economy.

2022

Industry Specialization

Expanded our team with industry specialists in technology, healthcare, finance, and marketing. Launched specialized services for different professional sectors.

2025

AI-Enhanced Writing

Leading the industry by combining human expertise with AI tools for better keyword optimization and content enhancement, while maintaining the personal touch that makes resumes stand out.
